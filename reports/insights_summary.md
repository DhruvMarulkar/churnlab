# Customer Churn Dashboard – Key Insights

## Overview
This report summarizes insights from a machine learning–driven customer churn analysis and retention strategy simulation.

## Key Insights
- Customers are segmented into Low, Medium, and High churn risk groups.
- Medium-risk customers contribute the highest total revenue at risk.
- High-risk customers, while fewer, represent significant churn probability.

## Retention Strategy Analysis
- Multiple retention strategies were simulated:
  - Discount offers
  - Contract upgrades
  - Tech support addition
- Strategy comparison shows clear differences in average churn probability.
- The best-performing strategy results in the lowest predicted churn.

## Business Recommendation
Prioritize targeted retention strategies for high- and medium-risk customers to reduce churn and protect annual revenue.
